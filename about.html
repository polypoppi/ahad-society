the society.

ahad society is a weekend collective dedicated to intentional gatherings. we take the friction out of your days off by curating weekly programs designed for connection, creativity, and unwinding.

this isn't about packing into massive, noisy venues or navigating chaotic schedules. it is about well-planned spaces, genuine people, and taking a breather from the rush of the week. we handle the logistics; you simply arrive.

01

unplug.

disconnect from the noise. our physical spaces are designed to help you step away from the screen, reset, and be present.

02

create.

hands-on workshops and creative sessions. whether it's pottery, painting, or journaling. no prior experience needed.

03

grow.

learn and experience new things entirely at your own pace. our spaces are curated for low-pressure exploration.
